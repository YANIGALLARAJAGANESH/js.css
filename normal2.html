Fila Holdings Corp. is a South Korean sportswear brand with Italian roots. The company originally was founded by Ettore and Giansevero Fila in 1911 in Coggiola, near Biella, Italy.[2] In 2003, it was sold to United States-based Sports Brand International.[3] Fila Korea acquired the brand in 2007 and launched its initial public offering on the Korea Exchange in September 2010.[4][5][6] Fila Holdings owns golf equipment maker Acushnet Company. Fila Holdings' largest shareholders include Piemonte Co., Ltd at around 20%, Fila Holdings at 20%, and South Korea's National Pension Service at around 13%.[7] Gene Yoon (Yoon Yoon-su), who owns a 75% stake in Piemonte,[8] serves as the chairman of Fila Holdings. The chief executive officer is Yoon Keun-chang.
